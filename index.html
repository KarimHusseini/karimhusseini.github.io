About

I am a PhD student at the University of Illinois Chicago, fortunate enough to be working with Professors Mesrob Ohannessian, Xinhua Zhang, and Ian Kash. My research focuses on RLxLLMs and intelligence research, with a particular interest RLHF and reasoning. I am passionate about model alignment and enhancing model capabilities, and work on developing RL training algorithms to achieve those goals. I remain interested in and am inspired by the areas of information theory, optimization, algorithm design, algorithmic complexity theory, and neuroscience.

Prior to my PhD, I obtained my bachelor's in Computer and Communications engineering at the American University of Beirut.
